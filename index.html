Meet Leng Cheaheng

Hi, I'm Leng Cheaheng. I was born and raised in Siem Reap. I grew up exploring the temples of Angkor as a kid, and my grandparents used to tell me stories about the Khmer kings and the people who built them. That curiosity stuck with me.

I have been driving and guiding for over 12 years. My favourite part of the job is still watching someone see Angkor Wat at sunrise for the first time. That moment never gets old.

Driving is how I take care of my family. My wife, my daughter, and our baby boy on the way. Every trip I take, I give it everything. When you book with me, you are supporting a real family, not a company.

I speak fluent English, I know every back road and hidden spot, and I always show up on time. My car is clean and the AC is strong. But mostly I just want you to enjoy Siem Reap as much as I do.

The Wonders of Angkor

Must Visit

Angkor Wat

The biggest religious monument in the world. I recommend getting there by 4:45 AM for the sunrise over the reflection pool. We will look around the bas-reliefs while most tourists are still having breakfast.

The Stone Faces

Bayon Temple

Inside Angkor Thom, Bayon has 216 giant stone faces carved into its towers. The morning light hits the sandstone in a way that is hard to describe. Bring a camera.

Nature's Embrace

Ta Prohm

Ta Prohm was left mostly as it was found. Huge tree roots grow right through the walls. It is unlike anything else you will see.
